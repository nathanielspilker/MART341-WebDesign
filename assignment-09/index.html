How to Play the New Game Eldenring

Instructions

Step 1 - Gather Materials

  • The game
  • A TV
  • A console to play it on



Step 2 - Start up the Game and Make a Character

Press the new game button. After this, many different classes will appear. Each has a unique name and unique abillities. All classes are fun and unique. Once ready, click submit.

Try out any class. They're all fun!




Step 3 - Play the Game!

Now comes the fun part, just play the game! All you need to do is have fun exploring a rich and beutiful world. Defeat evil demi-gods, find new exciting locations, and aquire fun and unique skills and equipment. Enjoy the game!
