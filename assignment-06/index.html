Spaghetti Marinara

ingridients

  • angel hair pasta
  • 1 cup of marinara
  • 2tsp garlic salt

Directions

  1. Preheat the stove to 400oF.
  2. Wait for the water to boil before adding pasta!
  3. Cook the pasta in the water for 7 minutes stirring regularly.
  4. Strain pasta to remove water.
  5. Cook marinara at 200oF for 4 minutes or until warm.
  6. Add pasta, marinara, and salt into bowl.
